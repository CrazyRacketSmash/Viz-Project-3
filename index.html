Lost Character Analysis

Lost aired on the American Broadcasting Company (ABC) from 2004 to 2010, running for 6 seasons and 121 episodes. The series follows survivors of a plane crash stranded on a mysterious island filled with strange and supernatural elements.

It features a diverse cast of characters, including Jack Shephard, Kate Austen, James "Sawyer" Ford, and John Locke, whose backstories are revealed throughout the show.

Blending mystery, drama, science fiction, and adventure, Lost became a cultural phenomenon known for its complex storytelling and unexpected twists.
